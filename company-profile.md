With 20 years of production expertise, Guangzhou Taoying Technology Co., Ltd. (formerly Chaoyang Trading Co., Ltd.) is a professional garment manufacturer and exporter. Integrating industry and trade, the company operates its own garment factory supported by a team of experienced technical professionals. We specialize in a full range of products including denim series, casual pants, sweaters, and various knitted apparel. Adhering to the principle of "Customer First, Quality Supreme", we implement a strict standardized Quality Management System throughout production, ensuring all products pass rigorous quality inspections.

## Core Service Guarantees

- **Quality Guarantee:** Our owned factory maintains high and stable production capacity, ensuring consistent and reliable product quality.
- **Supply Chain Guarantee:** Our professional team proactively sources new fabrics and market trends, guaranteeing a resilient and innovative supply chain.
- **Design & Development Guarantee:** We possess strong in-house design and R&D capabilities, providing customers with diverse style options and completing samples within 14 days based on requirements.
